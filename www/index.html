How to play

Controls:

  • Move your mouse or touch to control the gravity field.
  • Use Arrow Keys to nudge the gravity field with the keyboard.
  • Press L or Space to toggle pointer lock (keyboard control).
  • Press H to open this help overlay.

Collect targets, avoid obstacles, and use boosters. Keyboard controls and a help overlay improve accessibility for non-pointer users.
